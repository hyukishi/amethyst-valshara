Why this layout

            
This client keeps the moment-to-moment controls close at hand, moves the raw terminal into a summonable tool panel, and gives the map, chat, combat, and inventory their own breathing room.

            
It also keeps account and character creation in the browser through the game’s live login flow, so the UI stays aligned with your real server logic.

          

          

            
Tips

            
    
              
  • Use ?debug in the URL to open the terminal by default.
    • 
              
  • Tab in the command box cycles local completions from exits, aliases, items, and skills.
    • 
              
  • The combat pane stays readable even if the raw terminal is minimized.
